Cardiff Castle

Step into history at this iconic castle, featuring Roman walls, a Norman keep, and Victorian interiors.

National Museum Cardiff

Explore art, history, and natural sciences in this free museum, perfect for culture enthusiasts.

Bute Park

Relax in this sprawling green space with riverside walks, gardens, and historic landmarks.

Cardiff Bay

Enjoy waterfront views, dining, and attractions like the Wales Millennium Centre.

Engage kids with hands-on science exhibits at this interactive museum in Cardiff Bay.

Go behind the scenes of Cardiff’s famous stadium, a hit for sports-loving families.
