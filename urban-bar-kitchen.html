Dining Experience

Taste the Moment

At Urban Bar & Kitchen, we serve everything from tasty tapas to juicy lamb, perfect for sharing, snacking, or a full meal. Whether it’s a quick bite, drinks with friends, or a laid-back dinner, our vibrant atmosphere and delicious dishes make every visit memorable. Don’t miss our seasonal afternoon tea, packed with fresh savouries, sweet bites, and your choice of tea or bubbles.
