Meetings & Events

Host Impactful Events

Host impactful events in the heart of the city at Cardiff Holland House. With 17 versatile meeting and conference rooms including the impressive Calon Suite, our spaces are designed for flexibility, function and inspiration. Whether you are planning a board meeting, large scale conference, gala dinner or sports team stay, our dedicated events team will ensure a seamless experience from start to finish.
