Getting Here

Travel to Cardiff Holland House

Exit M4 at Junction 33 (signposted Cardiff West / Cardiff Bay / A4232)

Follow signs to A4232 Cardiff City Centre

Continue on A4232 (dual carriageway) for about 7.5 miles

Take the exit for Cardiff Bay / Cardiff City Centre

At the roundabout, take the 2nd exit onto A4160 (signposted City Centre)

Continue straight onto Callaghan Square and then Bute Terrace

Turn left at the lights onto Fitzalan Place

Continue onto Newport Road

The hotel will be on your right hand side

Exit M4 Junction 29 onto A48(M) towards Cardiff (East & South)

Continue on A48 (M) which becomes Eastern Avenue (A48)

Exit at the A4161 / Cardiff (East) exit

Follow signs to the City Centre (A4161/Newport Road)

Continue along Newport Road for about 2 miles

The hotel is on the left hand side, just past Fitzalan Place.

Nearest bus & train station – Cardiff Central – 0.9 miles / 20 minutes walk / 11 minutes drive
